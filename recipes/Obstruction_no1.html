Obstruction no.1

Recipe

  • 2 oz Bourbon (Buffalo Trace)
  • .75 oz Walnut Liqueur
  • 2 bar spoons maple syrup
  • 3-4 dashes Angostura bitters

Building Steps

  • combine ingredients in mixing glass
  • add ice and stir till chilled and diluted
  • Strain into rocks glass with large rock
  • smoke that bad boy, if you can
